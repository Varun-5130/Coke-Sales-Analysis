🥤 Coke Sales Analysis


📌 Project Overview

The Coke Sales Analysis project focuses on analyzing beverage sales data to uncover trends, evaluate performance across regions and brands, and generate actionable business insights.

The analysis is performed using Microsoft Excel, leveraging pivot tables, charts, and data visualization techniques to support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Objectives

Analyze monthly sales trends to identify seasonal patterns

Compare regional sales performance

Evaluate brand-wise contribution to total revenue

Measure retailer contribution to overall sales

Provide strategic recommendations for business improvement

📂 Dataset

The dataset contains structured sales transaction data including:

Retailer

Brand

Region

Units Sold

Revenue

Profit

Date / Month

The project includes raw data and multiple pivot-based summaries for detailed analysis.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Microsoft Excel

Pivot Tables

Pivot Charts

Data Cleaning Techniques

Business Intelligence Concepts

📊 Key Analysis Performed
📅 Month-wise Analysis

Evaluated sales trends across different months to identify high and low performance periods.

🌍 Region-wise Analysis

Compared revenue and sales contribution across regions.

🧃 Brand Performance Analysis

Measured brand-wise revenue contribution and overall performance trends.

🛒 Retailer Contribution Analysis

Analyzed retailer-level sales contribution to identify key partners.

📊 Cross-Analysis

Brand vs Month

Brand vs Region
To understand seasonal and geographic preferences.

🔍 Insights

Sales show noticeable seasonal patterns.

Regional performance varies significantly.

Certain brands contribute a higher share of revenue.

Retailer partnerships strongly impact total sales.

📌 Recommendations

Strengthen marketing efforts during low-performing periods.

Focus on expanding distribution in underperforming regions.

Promote high-performing products strategically.

Build stronger collaboration with key retail partners.
